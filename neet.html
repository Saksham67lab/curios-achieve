The Exam That Crushes Millions of Dreams (NEET)

INTRODUCTION Every year millions of Indian students prepare for that exam to begin their journey for becoming an doctor. It is considered as the biggest exam of India, This exam is held by an Indian government institute NTA, I am talking about the biggest exam of India NEET. According to National Testing Agency (NTA). This exam is originally as usual held on May 3 2026, But for some reasons this exam is cancelled. WHAT IS NEET NEET is the India one of the biggest exam, this exam is conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA). It is one of the most important exam for many NEET aspirants, That exam decide the future of millions of students, this exam is the first step toward becoming a doctor. It is considered the largest medical examination in India. NEET was proposed in 2010 as a common medical entrance examination for Indian students, It was planned for 2012 although it was postponed to 2013 and the first NEET exam was held on 5 May 2013. Why is NEET Important ? Millions of students appear for this examination for as a hope to complete their dream as to become a doctor. It is the gateway to medical colleges across. Many students spends years preparing for NEET through self study and coaching, This examination held once a year, A good NEET score can help students get admission to top government medical colleges. Many families sold their properties for their child education and only they have a hope left that their child clear NEET and become a doctor. Main Issues and Controversy NEET exam was planned to held on May 3 2026 but before the exam, the question paper of NEET was leaked and after students gave exam the exam was cancelled and postponed to 21 June 2026 and the students were not satisfied with that decision because the students who studied all the year and get good marks and heard about reexamination they were angry and not satisfied because they cannot trust that the next exam could be equally difficult or even more difficult. Some people alleged that there is failure in security in the examination process. The controversy also led to various allegations and debates on social media, raising questions about the security of the examination process. Impact On Students Students spend years for preparing for NEET, Mostly students take expensive coaching, That controversy caused stress and anxiety among many students, An reexam means students have to study again in that pressure again, Most students lost trust from Indian examination system, Parents were also affected emotionally and financially because of reexamination students have to take expensive coaching again and study again and they have a lot of pressure and a lot of competition, also students worried about the delays in admission and their future careers. Solution NTA should improve the security of question paper to prevent leaks, Strict punishment should be given to who involved in question paper leaks, Strong rules should be build to maintain the fairness for all students, Government should conduct investigation for those who are involved in exam leak, they have to encrypt the question paper before exam starts, They have to focus on fairness, transparency, and equal opportunity for all candidates. The government and NTA should work together to rebuild the trust. Conclusion NEET is the most important exam for medical aspirants, Millions of students depend on it and it decided their future journey. The NEET 2026 was the very controversial exam because of it question paper leaked and for about exam cancelled and reexamination mostly students were not satisfied with that decision and The controversy raised serious questions about the examination process and the decisions taken by the authorities.
