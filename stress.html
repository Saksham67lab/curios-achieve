Why Students Are More Stressed Than Before?

Introduction Student life has changed a lot in recent years. Education has become more competitive as compared to previous generation. Stress has become a common issue likely if there is 5 students so the most likely 3 students are under stress because of study and for the family pressure. In todays time students struggle to balance the study and personal time as for study students has to sacrificed their passion and their hobby to compete with other students. ACADEMIC COMPETITION In today's time the completion is very high, More students are competing for limited college and good education and for jobs seats. The students are more than the college and jobs seats. So they have to sacrifice their personal life for high score and for good education and job seats. The fear of failing the exam creates stress and anxiety and some are go in depression, They feel pressure to score higher than others. Parental and Family Expectation Parents want that their students score high, In that expectation sometimes its leads to child stress, anxiety or sometimes depression. Many parents compared their child with his/her siblings or cousins and sometimes with your classmates. Students may worry about disappoint their parents. Students compare their life with others on social media and see others achieving their dream. Lack Of Free Time And Hobbies Students spend many hours in school and coaching, and at home they have to complete their school assignment and coaching homework, A average student spend around half day in coaching and after reaching home they cannot rest and have to complete their assignments, They have to sacrifice their sleep, their passion and hobbies to just being become an average student, They didn't have time to play outdoor game, they are already exhausted. After completing the assignment that take long time they have to revise, practice and do self study. Fear Of Failure Many students thought if they score bad in exam that can ruin their future, The fear of getting bad in exam create anxiety and stress, They fear to disappoint their parents and many parents scared their child so they didn't even thought of that and they afraid to taking risk and become better. Failure often seen by them as end rather than an opportunity to learn or do something better, Constant fear reduced their confidence and motivation, For making proud their parents students are sometimes mentally exhausted. Effect Of Stress On Students Stress effect both physical and mental health and, It effect the mental state of a student. Students struggle to concentrate on the studies, And sometimes stress leads to depression, In depression person didn't like to talk with people and cannot interact with anyone so easily. Stress affect on sleep to, It affect the sleep cycle also. Students feel tired, frustrated or emotionally exhausted, And it also change the nature of a person and its effects on its personal life also. Solution Students should create a study schedule and also they can divide their big tasks into smaller tasks that help in reducing pressure of doing it in once. Students can also do exercise or yoga that can reduce their stress. They can do hobbies such as reading, sports, music and drawing, etc. Their parents should encourage them rather than pressuring them. Their parents should also listen them and give them an opportunity to choose their career. Students also have friends or anybody that can talk with him/her openly.
