The Last Train At Midnight

The station was silent. Only the cold wind moved through the empty platforms.

Arjun checked his watch. 12:00 AM. Exactly midnight.

No trains were scheduled. Yet a distant whistle echoed through the darkness.

A black train slowly emerged from the fog. No lights. No passengers. No sound.

The doors opened. Arjun stepped inside. And the moment he did, everything changed.
